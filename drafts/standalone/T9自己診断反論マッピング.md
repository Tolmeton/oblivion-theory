## Rebuttal Templates

### R1: "Your framework is unfalsifiable"

> The T9 framework (§3.6) strictly subsumes Popper's falsifiability criterion. Falsifiability corresponds to a single $U_{\mathrm{causal}} \dashv N_{\mathrm{causal}}$ pair. T9 requires detectability and recoverability for *every* structural level (Eq. T9). The paper applies T9 reflexively: 7 concrete $U_i$ are diagnosed, with explicit recovery operations. Three are already executed ($U_{\mathrm{proxy}}$, $U_{\mathrm{static}}$, $U_{\mathrm{temporal}}$), and four are specified with independently testable predictions. To demand falsifiability alone is to commit $U_{\mathrm{adjoint}}$ — seeing only the refutation side.

### R2: "Negative results undermine claims"

> No. Each negative result is a *structurally informative diagnostic* under T9:
> - **§5.6** $U_{\mathrm{static}}$: the refuted $T_{\mathrm{rot}}$ delay prediction was replaced by a strictly more informative dynamic-range prediction ($r_s = +0.64$, $p < 10^{-14}$)
> - **§7.3** $U_{\mathrm{dilution}}$: the CoT degradation ($\rho$: 0.249 → 0.203) demonstrates that natural-language redescription injects noise morphisms, sharpening the theory's distinction between structural and surface augmentation
> A framework that treats all negative results as "failures" commits $U_{\mathrm{adjoint}}$.

### R3: "Probing shows correlation, not causation"

> Acknowledged (§7.1, L1077). This is explicitly diagnosed as $U_{\mathrm{causal}}$. Three concrete recovery operations are specified: (1) ablation of MCP servers, (2) counterfactual context manipulation, (3) controlled composition-only training. These are independently falsifiable and constitute a concrete research programme. A framework that *identifies its causal gap and specifies recovery* is T9-sound.

### R4: "HGK measurement is circular"

> Acknowledged (§7.9, L1600). This is $U_{\mathrm{HGK}}$ — designer-circularity forgetting. Three recovery operations: replicate on external systems, derive logically independent predictions, ablate HGK components. The §7.7.4 falsifiable predictions are instances of $N_{\mathrm{external}}^{\mathrm{predict}}$, partially executed with statistical validation (P3: $\rho = 0.52$, $p = 0.007$).

### R5: "The φ decomposition assumes layer independence"

> Acknowledged (§7.7.4, L1373). This is $U_{\mathrm{independence}}$. Empirical P1 test shows $r = 0.28$ ($p = 0.27$), confirming no covariance in the observed regime. The recovery operation — causal intervention modifying $\varphi_0$ while holding composition constant — is specified.
